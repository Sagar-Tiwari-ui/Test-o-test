Preparation Strategy

Time Management

  • Dedicate 8-10 hours daily for studies
  • Follow 50/10 rule: 50 minutes study, 10 minutes break
  • Allocate time based on your strengths and weaknesses
  • Keep 2 hours daily for revision
  • Solve at least one mock test every week

Study Plan for JEE Mains

  • Complete NCERT thoroughly
  • Focus on MCQ practice
  • Regular mock tests
  • Chapter-wise problem solving
  • Quick revision techniques

Additional Preparation for Advanced

  • Focus on multi-concept problems
  • Practice previous year papers
  • Emphasis on theoretical understanding
  • Advanced level problem solving
  • Time management strategies

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Neglecting NCERT concepts

  • Not analyzing mistakes in tests
  • Last-minute preparation

Mental Health and Wellness Tips

  • Maintain a healthy sleep schedule (7-8 hours)
  • Include physical activity in daily routine
  • Practice meditation or relaxation techniques

  • Maintain a balanced diet
  • Stay connected with family and friends
